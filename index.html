// FOUR MODES
NORMAL · AUTO · HELL · TRAIN
AUTO

Exhaustive search

For every rotation × column (40 placements) the AI scores the resulting board and plays the max — sub-millisecond at x86 speeds.

HELL MODE

Adversarial pieces

A one-level minimax: the generator picks the piece that minimizes the AI's best achievable score.

TRAIN

(1+1)-ES hill climber

Perturb one weight, self-play N games, keep the change only if the average score improves.

BOARD

Bit-packed rows

10×20 grid as 20 words; a full row is one CMP reg, 0x3FF instruction.
